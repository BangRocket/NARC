Entity Types:
-------------

Neutral
	Can be shot, won´t attack player
	Once attacked by player, neutral will turn into enemy and will attack the player

Enemy
	Can be shot, will attack player

Turret
	Can be shot, will attack player

Ammo
	Can be collected




Movement Types:
---------------

0 - stationary, not moving (e.g. Ammo or "stuff" around level)
1 - stationary, direction follows player (e.g. Turret)
2 - AutoMovement (e.g. Enemy or Neutral)
