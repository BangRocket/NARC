Supported audio formats:

SFX & Music
	Ogg Vorbis


Other audio formats can be enabled in "SFX_SDL.hpp"...

Why I prefer Ogg Vorbis:

	- license free
	- small file sizes
	- fast decoding


The audio-engine actually works with

	Samplerate=22050
	StereoChannels=2
	ChunkSize=2048
	MaxChannels=64


configured in "./Data/AudioConfg.ini"
